Since 2023/11/01 pwaos.net has announced to join the Metaverse Standards Forum as the first participant from the pwa technology industry, will contribute the pwa technology in the working group of 3D Web Interoperability. The Metaverse Standards Forum is a newly established governing body that aims to promote alignment on priorities and requirements for Metaverse interoperability standards.
